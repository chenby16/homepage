About me

I'm an assistant professor in Department of Mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University from Oct 2024. I worked in Yau Mathematical Sciences Center in Tsinghua University as a postdoc from Sept 2021-Sept 2024 and my postdoctoral mentor was Caucher Birkar. I graduated from Department of Mathematical Sciences in Tsinghua University in July 2021, under the supervision of Stephen Shing-Toung Yau.

I am interested in birational geometry and singularity theory, especially minimal model program, boundedness results in birational geometry, the theory of complements, the moduli theory of varieties of general type and of non-negative Kodaira dimension, applications of the theory of mixed Hodge modules.
