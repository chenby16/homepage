About me

I'm a graduated student postdoc in Department of Mathematical Sciences in Tsinghua University working in algebraic geometry. My supervisor is Stephen Shing-Toung Yau .

I am interested in invariants of singularities, including

  • birational invariants, such as minimal log discrepancies, log canonical thresh- olds, multiplier ideals,
  • algebraic invariants, such as Milnor algebra, Tjurina algebra, the module of derivations,
  • invariants arsing from the theory of D-modules, such as Bernstein-Sato polynomials, vanishing cycles, Hodge ideals,
    • and their relationships.
