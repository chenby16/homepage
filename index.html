About me

I'm a postdoc in Yau Mathematical Sciences Center, Tsinghua University. My postdoctoral mentor is Caucher Birkar. I graduated from Department of Mathematical Sciences in Tsinghua University in July 2021, under supervision of Stephen Shing-Toung Yau.

I am interested in birational geometry and singularity theory, especially boundedness results in birational geometry, the moduli theory of varieties of general type and of non-negative Kodaira dimension, applications of the theory of mixed Hodge modules.
